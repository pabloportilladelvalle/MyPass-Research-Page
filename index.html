Our Vision

Our Vision

To help residents control and share their identity documents through a ditributed ledger, facilitating their access to social and health services, especially those vital to ending homelessness such as housing, public benefits and access to medical care.

Autonomy

Your identity,
your documents

As the primary victims of identity theft and identity fraud, our vision is to help the homeless community regain full control over their identity without the worry of loosing the vital documents they depend on for public services.

MyPass is a secure document storage solution designed to help people store and share IDs and documents digitally with service providers.

An identity storage solution

How does it work?

1
Upload

User uploads their document into the MyPass platform.

2
Share

User shares the document with contacts in their network.

3
Notarize

If a notarized copy is needed, a licensed notary can stamp, sign and upload it.

4
Verify

Verifiers use an external site to successfully verify the document's credentials.

What's the benefit of using MyPass?

Conventional

In-person and impractical, physical documents can only be verified when the client is present.
Traditional Approach
  • Easily lost or damaged
  • Must be shared in person, one person at a time
  • Cannot be duplicated or altered
  • Can be signed, stamped and notarized

With MyPass

Digitally certified and encrypted, MyPass allows for the decentralized distribution of certified documents, allowing for remote verification and secure shareability.
Mypass Approach
  • Cannot be lost or damaged
  • Can be shared remotely, with multiple parties
  • Cannot be duplicated or altered
  • Can be signed, stamped and notarized

MyPass Users

Three User Types

MyPass has two main user types: Helpers and Owners. Three counting the platform's administrator(s).

Owner Heroku Helper Heroku

Document Owner

Document Owners are the heart of MyPass. These are the users that wish to upload, store and safekeep their own digital documents.

Login Page

Document Helper

Document Helpers are case managers, relatives or advocates of Document Owners. They exist to help upload and notarize their documents

Login Page

Administrator

MyPass Administrators are the architects of the platform. They adapt the application to the contextual needs of where MyPass exists. Responsibilities include setting up document types, delineating role permissions and toggling features.
